## Leadership and Community Roles

Leadership, for me, has never been about titles — it has been about showing up, serving faithfully, and helping others grow.

## Current Roles

### Financial Secretary — Santa Rosa De Lima Choir and Ngong Cathedral Brass Band
**2023 to Present**

Managing the financial affairs of two active music communities under the Catholic Archdiocese of Nairobi. Responsibilities include budget planning, financial record-keeping, and ensuring transparent stewardship of community funds.

### Organizing Secretary — iLEAD Fellowship Program (Cohort 9)
**2022 to Present, Emerging Leaders Foundation-Africa**

Coordinating the administration and logistics of the iLEAD Fellowship. Responsibilities include scheduling, communication with fellows and facilitators, and supporting delivery of the programme curriculum.

### Chairperson — Kenswed Secondary School Alumni Association
**2019 to Present**

Leading the alumni association of my former secondary school, driving initiatives that reconnect graduates and channel resources back into the school community.

## Past Roles

### Peace Ambassador and Peer Counsellor — Maseno University
**2017 to 2020**

Certified by Maseno University, serving as a student resource for conflict resolution, guidance, and mental wellness support.

### Chairperson — Catholic Students Association, Maseno University
**2018 to 2019**

Led the Catholic Students Association at Maseno University, overseeing spiritual formation activities and community service projects. Concurrently served as Vice-Chairperson of St. Anne's Chaplaincy Council.

### Chairperson, Development Committee — St. Anne's Chaplaincy, Maseno University
**2017 to 2018**

Chaired the development committee responsible for infrastructure and programmatic growth within St. Anne's Chaplaincy.

### Treasurer — Christian Union, Kenswed Secondary School
**2014 to 2015**

Managed the finances of the school Christian Union society, building early skills in financial accountability and trust stewardship.

# Josiah K. Kimani — Personal Website

## About Me

I am a Google DeepMind Scholar (2024–2025) pursuing an MSc in Mathematical Sciences, specialising in Artificial Intelligence for Science, at the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS) South Africa, accredited by Stellenbosch University.

My research sits at the intersection of machine learning and atmospheric science. My thesis, **PiggyCast**, explores a stacking-based ensemble AI approach to improve weather prediction accuracy — contributing to the broader challenge of climate adaptation across Africa.

Beyond research, I am passionate about techno-democracy, responsible AI governance, and the role technology can play in driving social good across Kenya and Africa.
